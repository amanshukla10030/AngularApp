Automotive Manufacturing Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Assembly Line Visit

Morning: Arrival at automotive manufacturing plant. Safety briefing and equipment distribution. Guided tour through the main assembly line, witnessing the complete vehicle manufacturing process from raw materials to finished products.

Afternoon: Visit to quality control department and testing facilities. Interactive session with plant engineers. Lunch at the facility canteen with management team. Evening departure to hotel.

Day 2: Technology & Innovation Center

Morning: Visit to R&D center and technology innovation lab. Demonstration of latest manufacturing technologies and automation systems. Q&A session with senior engineers and technology experts.

Afternoon: Tour of the paint shop and final assembly area. Certificate distribution ceremony. Departure with comprehensive understanding of modern automotive manufacturing.
