Maa Sharda Darshan Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Maihar – Evening Darshan

Arrival: Upon arrival at Maihar or nearby Satna/Jabalpur railway station, our representative will receive you and transfer you to your hotel in Maihar. After check-in and some rest, head for evening darshan at Maa Sharda Temple, situated atop the Trikuta Hill. You can either trek 1,063 steps or use the ropeway (if operational) for a comfortable ascent. Experience the spiritual aura and enjoy the panoramic view of the town below. Dinner & Overnight Stay at Hotel.

Day 2: Morning Puja & Nearby Sightseeing

Early Morning: Begin your day with a morning puja and darshan at the Maa Sharda Temple. After breakfast, explore nearby spiritual sites like the Alha Udal Akhada, believed to be associated with the legendary warriors who were devotees of Maa Sharda. You may also visit Badi Mai Temple and Oila Temple, or relax at Barhi Talab. In the evening, you can again visit the temple for aarti or simply enjoy a peaceful walk through the town. Dinner & Overnight Stay at Hotel.

Day 3: Departure from Maihar

After breakfast: Check out from your hotel and proceed to your departure point at Maihar / Satna / Jabalpur Railway Station, with the divine blessings of Maa Sharda and cherished spiritual memories.

Chitrakoot Darshan Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Chitrakoot – Leisure & Spiritual Orientation

Arrival at Chitrakoot Dham (Karwi) or Satna Railway Station: Our representative will welcome you and transfer you to the hotel in Chitrakoot. After check-in and some rest, take a leisurely walk to get acquainted with the spiritual ambiance of this divine town nestled along the banks of the Mandakini River. In the evening, attend the Sandhya Aarti at Ram Ghat, a sacred place where Lord Rama, Sita, and Lakshmana are believed to have spent time during their exile. Dinner & Overnight Stay at Hotel.

Day 2: Full-Day Chitrakoot Darshan – Ramayana Trail

After breakfast: Begin your full-day pilgrimage tour of the key Ramayana sites in Chitrakoot. Visit the sacred Kamadgiri Temple, believed to be the heart of Chitrakoot and the place where Lord Rama resided during his exile. Continue to Bharat Milap Temple, the sacred spot where Bharat met Lord Rama after 14 years of exile. Explore the Gupt Godavari Caves, two sacred streams emerging from a cave believed to be used by Lord Rama and Sita. Visit Hanuman Dhara, a picturesque site with a steep climb and a waterfall said to be created by Lord Rama for Hanuman's comfort. Dinner & Overnight Stay at Hotel.

Day 3: Nearby Excursions & Peaceful Exploration

After breakfast: Enjoy a quieter day visiting lesser-known but spiritually rich sites. Visit Sati Anusuya Ashram, the hermitage where Sita was raised, and explore the peaceful surroundings. Spend time by the Mandakini River or visit Janaki Kund, Spatik Shila (a white rock believed to bear the footprints of Lord Rama and Sita), and Valmiki Ashram. Evening at leisure or optional revisit to Ram Ghat for the aarti. Dinner & Overnight Stay at Hotel.

Day 4: Departure from Chitrakoot

After breakfast: Check out from the hotel and proceed to Chitrakoot Dham / Satna Railway Station for your return journey, carrying divine blessings and tranquil memories of this sacred land of Lord Rama.
