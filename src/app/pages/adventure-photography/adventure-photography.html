Wildlife Photography Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival at Wildlife Sanctuary

Arrival: Upon arrival at the nearest railway station, you'll be warmly welcomed by our representative and transferred to your wildlife resort. After check-in and freshening up, enjoy a relaxed afternoon soaking in the natural surroundings of the forest. Take a nature walk around the resort area or visit a nearby tribal village for a cultural glimpse. In the evening, enjoy a cozy bonfire (seasonal) and a wildlife documentary. Dinner & Overnight Stay at Hotel.

Day 2: Full-Day Wildlife Safari

Early Morning: Wake up early and head out for an exciting morning jungle safari in an open gypsy, accompanied by an expert naturalist. The wildlife sanctuary is famous for its population of Royal Bengal Tigers, Barasingha (swamp deer), leopards, wild dogs, and numerous bird species. Return to the resort for breakfast and a leisurely midday break. In the afternoon, head for a second safari to explore a different zone of the park, maximizing your chances of wildlife sightings. After an adventurous day, return to the resort to relax. Dinner & Overnight Stay at Hotel.

Day 3: Departure

After breakfast: Check out from the resort. You will be transferred back to the railway station for your onward journey, carrying unforgettable memories of your close encounters with the wild in the heart of the sanctuary.
