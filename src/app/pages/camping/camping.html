Wildlife Camping Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Camp Setup

Arrival: Upon arrival at the nearest railway station, you'll be warmly welcomed by our representative and transferred to the camping site. After setting up your tents and freshening up, enjoy a relaxed afternoon soaking in the natural surroundings of the forest. Take a nature walk around the camping area or visit a nearby tribal village for a cultural glimpse. In the evening, enjoy a cozy bonfire with traditional folk music and wildlife documentary. Dinner & Overnight Stay in Tents.

Day 2: Full-Day Wildlife Safari & Camping Activities

Early Morning: Wake up early and head out for an exciting morning jungle safari in an open gypsy, accompanied by an expert naturalist. The wildlife sanctuary is famous for its population of Royal Bengal Tigers, Barasingha (swamp deer), leopards, wild dogs, and numerous bird species. Return to the campsite for breakfast and enjoy camping activities like nature photography, bird watching, and forest trekking. In the afternoon, head for a second safari to explore a different zone of the park. Evening bonfire with stargazing session. Dinner & Overnight Stay in Tents.

Day 3: Departure

After breakfast: Pack up your camping gear and check out from the campsite. You will be transferred back to the railway station for your onward journey, carrying unforgettable memories of your wildlife camping experience.
