Historical Study Tour Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi - Arrival & Orientation

Arrival in Delhi: Welcome to the capital city! Upon arrival at Delhi Airport/Railway Station, our representative will transfer you to your hotel. After check-in and freshening up, attend an orientation session about the historical significance of the places you'll be visiting. Evening cultural program introducing India's diverse heritage. Dinner and overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 2: Delhi - Agra (Taj Mahal)

Delhi to Agra: After breakfast, drive to Agra, home of the magnificent Taj Mahal. Visit the iconic Taj Mahal at sunrise - a truly unforgettable experience. Explore Agra Fort and learn about Mughal architecture. Evening visit to Mehtab Bagh for sunset view of the Taj Mahal. Return to Agra. Dinner and overnight stay in Agra.

Day 3: Agra - Jaipur (Pink City)

Agra to Jaipur: After breakfast, drive to Jaipur, the Pink City. Visit City Palace, Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar (astronomical observatory), and local markets. Experience Rajasthani culture and cuisine. Evening traditional Rajasthani folk performance. Dinner and overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day 4: Jaipur - Delhi

Jaipur to Delhi: After breakfast, drive back to Delhi. En route, visit to Fatehpur Sikri, known for its magnificent architecture and historical significance. Upon arrival in Delhi, final group discussion and certificate distribution. Farewell dinner and overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 5: Departure from Delhi

Departure: After breakfast, transfer to Delhi Airport/Railway Station for your return journey, carrying with you enriching educational experiences and newfound knowledge about India's glorious past.
