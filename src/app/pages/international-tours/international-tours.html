European Explorer Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Paris - City of Lights

Arrival: Welcome to Paris, the romantic capital of France. Upon arrival at Charles de Gaulle Airport, our representative will transfer you to your hotel in the heart of the city. After check-in and freshening up, enjoy a leisurely evening stroll along the Champs-Élysées or visit the iconic Eiffel Tower. Dinner and overnight stay in Paris.

Day 2: Paris City Tour - Art & Culture

After breakfast: Explore the artistic treasures of Paris. Visit the world-renowned Louvre Museum, home to thousands of masterpieces including the Mona Lisa. Continue to Musée d'Orsay, impressive for its impressionist and post-impressionist collections. In the afternoon, visit Notre-Dame Cathedral, a masterpiece of Gothic architecture. Evening cruise along the Seine River with dinner at a riverside restaurant. Overnight stay in Paris.

Day 3: Day Trip to Versailles

Full-day excursion: Take a day trip to the magnificent Palace of Versailles, the former residence of French kings. Explore the opulent Hall of Mirrors, the vast gardens, and the Queen's Apartments. Learn about the fascinating history of the French monarchy and the Treaty of Versailles. Return to Paris in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay in Paris.

Day 4: Montmartre & Giverny - French Countryside

Day trip: Travel to the beautiful French countryside. Visit Montmartre, the artists' quarter with its charming streets and the Sacré-Cœur Basilica. Continue to Giverny, a picturesque medieval town known for its well-preserved architecture. Enjoy lunch at a traditional French restaurant. Return to Paris in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay in Paris.

Day 5: Departure from Paris

After breakfast: Enjoy a final Parisian breakfast at a local café. Last-minute shopping for souvenirs along the famous Rue de Rivoli. Transfer to Charles de Gaulle Airport for your return journey, carrying unforgettable memories of your European adventure.
