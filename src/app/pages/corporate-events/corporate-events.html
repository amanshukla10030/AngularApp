Team Building Retreat Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Welcome

Arrival: Upon arrival at the resort, check in and enjoy welcome refreshments. Meet your team and get acquainted with the retreat schedule. Evening welcome dinner and orientation session with team-building activities and icebreakers. Overnight stay at the resort.

Day 2: Team Building Activities

Full Day: Morning breakfast followed by intensive team-building workshops and problem-solving sessions. Lunch break. Afternoon continues with collaborative challenges and outdoor activities designed to enhance teamwork and communication skills. Evening dinner and entertainment program. Overnight stay at the resort.

Day 3: Skill Development & Departure

After breakfast: Final team-building activities and skill development sessions. Lunch and certificate distribution ceremony. Check out from the resort with enhanced team dynamics and new skills to implement in your workplace. Transfer to airport/railway station for departure.
